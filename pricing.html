Coaching Options

1-on-1 Coaching

This is my highest level of support. I work with you one-on-one to build fully personalized strength and mobility coaching around your body, your goals, and your life. We start with movement assessments, then I provide hands-on guidance, ongoing feedback, and programming that fits how you actually live.

  • →
    Movement assessments and personalized programming
  • →
    Hands-on technique coaching and form feedback
  • →
    Strength, mobility, posture, and injury prevention focus
  • →
    Nutrition guidance and recovery strategies
  • →
    Direct communication and consistent check-ins

Best for you if you want clarity, structure, and a coach who's deeply involved in your progress.

Hybrid Coaching

In-person + Online

I blend in-person sessions with structured online programming. You get accountability and hands-on coaching when we're together, plus the flexibility to stay consistent between sessions with programming I design for you.

  • →
    In-person movement assessments and hands-on coaching
  • →
    Online programming for consistency between sessions
  • →
    Structured strength and movement plan
  • →
    Accountability and guidance with flexibility

Best for you if you want in-person guidance without committing to full-time sessions.

Online Coaching & Programming

I deliver personalized training through an app, focused on education, structure, and the flexibility to train anywhere. You get a program built for you, with clear progressions and the understanding of why you're doing what you're doing.

  • →
    Personalized training program delivered through an app
  • →
    Strength, mobility, posture, and injury prevention focus
  • →
    Clear progressions and weekly structure
  • →
    Education and understanding of movement principles
  • →
    Optional check-ins and progress tracking

Best for you if you want smart training, consistency, and the freedom to train on your schedule.
